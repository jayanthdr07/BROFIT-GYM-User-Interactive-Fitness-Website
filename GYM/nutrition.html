Optimal Daily Nutrition Plan

?Breakfast (7:00 AM)

  • **Protein:** 4 Egg Whites and 1 Whole Egg Omelet
  • **Carbs:** 1/2 Cup Rolled Oats with Berries
  • **Fats:** Small handful of Almonds or Walnuts

Mid-Morning Snack (10:30 AM)

  • **Protein:** Whey Protein Shake (Mixed with water)
  • **Carbs:** 1 Apple or Banana

? Lunch (1:00 PM)

  • **Protein:** 150g Grilled Chicken Breast or Fish
  • **Carbs:** 1 cup Brown Rice or Quinoa
  • **Veggies:** Large mixed green salad or steamed broccoli

Pre-Workout Snack (4:30 PM)

  • **Carbs/Energy:** Small sweet potato or 1 slice whole-wheat toast with peanut butter

Post-Workout Snack (6:30 PM)

  • **Protein:** Whey Protein Shake (Mixed with milk or water)
  • **Carbs:** 1 Banana or 1 Rice Cake

Dinner (8:30 PM)

  • **Protein:** 150g Lean Steak or Tofu
  • **Veggies:** Large serving of roasted asparagus or green beans
**Important Note:** Drink at least 3-4 liters of water daily. Adjust portion sizes based on your individual weight, height, and activity level. Consult a nutritionist for personalized advice!
